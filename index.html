Welcome to Falcon Learning

Explore new and creative ways to sharpen your skills while enjoying the process.

Objective

Discover unorthodox techniques that boost listening, memory, and speaking while keeping things engaging and interactive.

Activities

Activity 1: Self-Diagnosis

Try to focus without support first. Write down what you catch, then compare with the original text. Finally, read aloud your corrections.

Activity 2: Roleplay Practice

Create short dialogues (like ordering coffee or asking directions). Act them out with a partner or practice by yourself aloud.

Activity 3: Linguistic Tools

Experiment with minimal pairs to sharpen your ear. Practice stress and intonation to see how meaning shifts.

Activity 4: Kinesthetic Encoding

Pair a physical movement with each new word. Move + Speak = Stronger memory link.

Reflection

Think about which methods worked best for you. Which ones will you keep practicing?
